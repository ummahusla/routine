# Zapier Chronological Timeline — 2011 to Present

> Raw research dossier. All quotes are verbatim from primary sources. Every fact includes a source URL.

---

## 2011 — Origins

**September 2011**
- Wade Foster and Bryan Helmig (colleagues at Veterans United Home Loans in Columbia, MO) begin developing a tool to integrate multiple software applications. Mike Knoop joins shortly after.
- Source: Wikipedia (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zapier)

**October 2011**
- **Columbia Startup Weekend:** All three founders attend. They pitch "API Mixer" — a tool to connect web application APIs without coding.
- Bryan Helmig: "We hacked all weekend, and I don't think we slept more than maybe six hours amongst all of us over the 54 hours."
- Wade Foster: "We built our MVP in 54 hours at Startup Weekend Columbia."
- **RESULT: WON the hackathon.** Zapier was the first business to win first place at Columbia Startup Weekend.
- Source: Taskade history (https://www.taskade.com/blog/zapier-history); Changelog (https://changelog.com/founderstalk/55)

**November 30, 2011**
- **First paying customer:** Andrew Warner (Mixergy podcaster) pays $100 via PayPal to Wade Foster's personal account for beta access to a Wufoo-to-AWeber integration the team built overnight.
- Warner's tweet: "I just paid $100 for a product it didn't even launch because @WadeFoster nailed what I was DESPERATE for."
- Source: Zapier blog (https://zapier.com/blog/anatomy-of-sale-or-how-andrew-warner-became-our-first-paying-user/)

**December 2011**
- Wade Foster leaves Veterans United to work on Zapier full-time.
- Waitlist: 10,000 users; integrations available: 25.
- Source: Wikipedia; Contrary Research (https://research.contrary.com/company/zapier)

---

## 2012 — Y Combinator and Public Launch

**Winter 2012**
- **First Y Combinator application: REJECTED** (W12 batch)
- Bryan Helmig: "The first time they just flat out rejected us. It was a nice email…"
- Source: Changelog (https://changelog.com/founderstalk/55)

**April 2012**
- Bryan Helmig leaves his job and joins Zapier full-time.

**May 2012**
- Mike Knoop joins Zapier full-time.
- **Zapier launches its public beta.** Founders relocate to Mountain View, CA for Y Combinator.
- **YC Summer 2012 batch (S12) acceptance.** Second application succeeded with traction: ~1,000 paying beta users, 10,000 on waitlist.
- Source: Wikipedia; Taskade history

**Summer 2012**
- YC batch. Bryan Helmig: "That summer we just worked; I don't think we slept a whole lot, we'd just work-work-work. We worked 16-hour days pretty regularly."
- Source: Changelog (https://changelog.com/founderstalk/55)

**August 2012**
- **Developer Platform launched.** Any app can now build a Zapier integration, exposing itself to Zapier's user base.
- YC blog announcement: "Zapier (YC S12) launches 3rd party developer platform - Now any app can zap data to 60+ services"
- Source: https://www.ycombinator.com/blog/zapier-yc-s12-launches-3rd-party-developer-pl

**October 31, 2012**
- **Seed round closes: $1.2 million** from Bessemer Venture Partners, Draper Fisher Jurvetson, Wufoo co-founder Kevin Hale, and seven angel investors.
- Platform at this point: 80+ web apps added since launch; 116 total services; premium accounts from $15/month.
- First formal employee hired: **Micah Bennett** (Chicago) for customer support.
- Source: TechCrunch (https://techcrunch.com/2012/10/31/zapier-raises-1-2m-seed-round/); Silicon Prairie News

## 2017 — Remote Culture Goes Public

**March 2017**
- **De-location package announced:** Zapier offers new employees up to $10,000 to move OUT of the San Francisco Bay Area.
- "if you're in the Bay Area and you take a job with Zapier we'll pay $10,000 to help you relocate to anywhere" — Wade Foster
- This generates enormous press coverage: Inc., FastCompany, QZ, CSMonitor, Deseret News, HR Dive, VOA Learning English, ABC7.
- Job applicants increase 53% after announcement.
- Ironically, by October 2017: no employees had actually taken the package.
- Source: YC blog (https://www.ycombinator.com/blog/the-ceo-who-pays-employees-to-de-locate-from-the-bay); FastCompany (https://www.fastcompany.com/40518203/zapiers-10000-de-location-offer-sparks-job-applicants-but-still-no-de-location)

**2017**
- 3 million users; $14.4M revenue (one source) / ~$25M+ ARR (other sources).
- HackerNoon interview published: Wade Foster announces "$35MM in ARR" and "grown 100% YoY."
- Note: The $35M figure from Bryan Helmig's Changelog episode and Wade Foster's HackerNoon interview likely reflects a specific date/time period.
- "Zapier for Teams" precursor products in development.
- Source: HackerNoon (https://hackernoon.com/founder-interviews-wade-foster-of-zapier-87de65e35246); Changelog (Founders Talk #55)

## 2021 — $5B Valuation, First Acquisition

**January 2021**
- **Secondary market transaction: Zapier valued at $5 billion.** Sequoia Capital and Steadfast Capital Management purchase shares from early investors. No new capital raised; founders do not sell.
- ARR at time of secondary: $140 million.
- Revenue multiple: 30.3x.
- Source: Wikipedia; The Information

**March 2021**
- **Forbes interview:** Alex Konrad interviews Wade Foster about the $5B valuation and Makerpad acquisition.
- Foster: "For us, we've always looked at financing events, whether they're primary, secondary or public markets, as a tool in the tool belt."
- Source: Forbes (via techmeme.com/210308/p25)

**March 2021**
- **Makerpad acquisition** — Zapier's FIRST acquisition.
- Target: Makerpad (no-code education service, 8,000+ members, ~$400K ARR).
- Ben Tossell (Makerpad founder): described as "a 'no-brainer'"
- Source: TechCrunch (https://techcrunch.com/2021/03/08/zapier-buys-no-code-focused-makerpad-in-its-first-acquisition/)

**2021**
- **"Interface Designer"** product launched (precursor to Interfaces).
- 7 million users; ~$140–165M ARR.
- Source: Taskade history; Startupgtm

---

## 2022 — AI Pivot Begins

**2022**
- Mike Knoop gives up executive CPO role; shifts focus to AI language model research ("went all in on AI language model research last summer").
- Zapier's AI pivot begins internally.
- 8 million users; ~$198–220M ARR.
- ~700 employees.
- Source: mikeknoop.com; Wikipedia

---

## 2023 — ZapConnect and Product Expansion

**January 2023**
- **Transfer by Zapier** launches — batch data processing tool for moving historical data between apps.
- Source: community.zapier.com/product-updates

**March 2023 — The "Code Red"**
- **GPT-4 launches.** Wade Foster declares company-wide "Code Red" — a term he'd never used before.
- "AI was here, and it was going to change everything."
- Company halts normal operations for a week-long AI hackathon.
- 360-person team generates 150 ideas and 60+ submissions.
- Pre-hackathon AI usage: ~10% of workforce.
- Source: Zapier blog (https://zapier.com/blog/how-zapier-rolled-out-ai/); Madrona (https://www.madrona.com/zapier-has-more-ai-agents-than-employees-heres-how-that-happened/)

**June 2023**
- New product features rolled out, including AI-powered field mapping.
- Source: Zapier blog (https://zapier.com/blog/new-product-features-june-2023/)

**ZapConnect 2023**
- **Zapier Tables** (previously in beta) released to general availability — free no-code database for all users.
- **Zapier Interfaces** launched publicly — no-code app builder.
- **Zapier Canvas** announced in alpha — AI-powered diagramming tool.
- **AI Chatbot** launched in beta.
- 1,253 integration improvements; platform reaches 6,000+ applications.
- Source: Zapier blog (https://zapier.com/blog/zapconnect-product-updates-2023/)

**September 28, 2023**
- Zapier Tables reaches **general availability**.
- Source: Taskade history

**Late 2023**
- Six months after "Code Red": **63% of Zapier employees use AI daily** (up from 10%).
- Source: Zapier blog (https://zapier.com/blog/how-zapier-rolled-out-ai/)

**2023**
- 10 million+ users; ~$250.7M ARR (14% YoY growth — slowdown from prior years).
- Source: Multiple

---

## 2024 — AI Products Launch, Acquisitions

**February 2025** (security incident, disclosed retroactively)
- Data breach via 2FA misconfiguration on employee account.
- Source: Taskade history article

**March 2024**
- **Vowel acquisition** (AI video conferencing team).
- Simultaneously: **Zapier Central launches** in public preview — AI workspace for building, teaching, and working with AI bots across 6,000+ apps.
- Andrew Berman (Vowel CEO) becomes Director of AI at Zapier.
- 388,000 Zapier customers using AI products at launch.
- Sheryl Soo: "The acquisition of Vowel enhances our AI capabilities and furthers our mission of democratizing AI and automation."
- Source: PR Newswire (https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/zapier-leads-the-evolution-of-ai-automation-with-acquisition-of-vowel-and-launch-of-zapier-central-302081740.html)

**April 2, 2024**
- **New Enterprise plan launched** — replacing previous "Company" plan. Key pricing changes:
  - Unlimited Zaps on all plans
  - Free plan reduced from 750 to 100 tasks/month
  - Pay-per-task billing enabled by default for new accounts
- Source: Multiple pricing sources

**Mid-2024**
- **Zapier Central evolves into Zapier Agents** — autonomous AI capabilities beyond simple automation.
- Source: Taskade history; multiple reviews

**July 2024**
- **NoCodeOps acquisition** — Atlanta-based no-code platform (had raised ~$5.5M from Hustle Fund and Charge Ventures).
- Source: Wikipedia (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zapier)

**End of 2024**
- AI adoption reaches **77%** of Zapier employees.
- ~$310M ARR (24% YoY growth).
- Source: Zapier blog

---

## 2025 — AI Orchestration Platform Identity

**October 23–27, 2025**
- Zapier conducts survey of 525 US C-Suite executives at 1,000+ employee companies.
- Key finding: "84% of Enterprises Plan to Boost AI Agent Investment"
- Source: Yahoo Finance (https://finance.yahoo.com/news/zapier-survey-finds-84-enterprises-130000504.html)

**October 2025**
- **Utopian Labs acquisition** (formerly Luna.ai) — AI sales email writing tool, Palo Alto, founded 2024.
- Utopian Labs operations ceased the following month.
- Source: Wikipedia (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zapier)

**Late 2025 — Chief People & AI Transformation Officer role created**
- The company's people function formally takes on AI transformation ownership.
- Source: Zapier blog (https://zapier.com/blog/how-zapier-rolled-out-ai/)

**ZapConnect 2025**
- Described as "the biggest evolution of the platform."
- **Copilot** launched — conversational AI assistant for building workflows via natural language.
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** support launched — connecting Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor to 30,000+ actions across 9,000+ apps.
- Tables and Interfaces made free on ALL plans (previously paid add-ons).
- **Enterprise Admin Hub** launched — centralized admin governance center.
- 32 new AI integrations added; total AI connections: 450+
- Chris Geoghegan, VP Product: "Copilot doesn't just help you build automations; it helps you find opportunities you never knew existed."
- Source: Yahoo Finance press release (https://finance.yahoo.com/news/zapier-adds-copilot-assistant-enterprise-130000698.html)

**AI adoption: 97%** of Zapier employees actively use AI in daily work.
> "More AI agents than human employees" — Zapier reportedly runs more deployed AI agents internally than it has human employees.
- Source: Madrona (https://www.madrona.com/zapier-has-more-ai-agents-than-employees-heres-how-that-happened/)

**November 2025**
- Shai Hulud 2.0 supply chain attack affects 425 Zapier NPM packages.
- Source: Taskade history

## December 2025 — Product Updates

From Zapier blog (https://zapier.com/blog/december-2025-product-updates/):

- **Admin Center redesign** — "All your admin controls in one place"
- **Adobe Workfront integration expansion** — new triggers/actions for project management
- **Workday Finance automation** — 9 new finance-focused triggers/actions
- **Zapier Agents enhancements:**
  - Draft/published versioning (beta) — safe iteration without affecting production agents
  - "Needs review" state for troubleshooting
- **MCP Tool Bundle Sharing** — share tool bundles via link
- **Human-in-the-Loop Guest Reviewers** — external collaborators can complete approval steps via secure links
- **Canvas Image Support** — drag-and-drop images in Canvas

## 2026 — Current State

From Zapier homepage (https://zapier.com/, fetched May 2026):

- **9,000+** apps integrated
- **66,000+** triggers and actions
- **81 billion** tasks automated since launch
- **3.39M+** MCP tool calls completed
- **593,138,971** AI tasks automated
- **450K+** Agents built
- **99.9%** uptime
- **$400M ARR** projected (29% growth from 2024)
- **800–1,221** employees across 38–40 countries

**Zapier's self-description (2026):** "the world's most connected AI platform"

Identity pivot complete: Zapier has repositioned from "workflow automation" to "AI orchestration platform" with Copilot, Agents, Chatbots, and MCP as the primary product surface.
