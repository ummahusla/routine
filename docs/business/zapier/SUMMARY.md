## 1. What Is Zapier

**Tagline (homepage, May 2026):** *"AI automation, governed"* — *"Your tools. Your rules. Any AI."*

Zapier is a **proprietary, cloud-only, no-code workflow automation platform** built around "Zaps" — workflows consisting of a trigger event in one application and one or more actions in connected apps. Founded in Columbia, Missouri in October 2011 by Wade Foster, Bryan Helmig, and Mike Knoop; officially launched through Y Combinator (S12) in 2012.

**Platform scale (May 2026):** 9,000+ apps, 66,000+ triggers/actions, 81B tasks automated, 3.4M+ companies, 593M+ AI tasks, 99.9% uptime.

**Vs. n8n / Make:** Zapier has the most integrations (9,000+ vs. n8n's ~1,500+ and Make's ~1,500+); is cloud-only (no self-hosting); charges per task (vs. n8n's per-workflow-run model); and has 450+ dedicated AI integrations. n8n positions itself as "1000x cheaper" for complex workflows by not multiplying cost per step.

## 2. Business Model & Pricing

Task-based SaaS. One successful action = one task. All plans include unlimited Zaps.

| Tier | Price | Tasks/mo |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | 100 |
| Professional | $19.99/mo | 750+ |
| Team | $69/mo | same tiers |
| Enterprise | Custom | Annual |

Tables and Interfaces free on all plans since ZapConnect 2025. Overage: 1.25x per task. Profitable since 2014. Churn below 5%.

## 6. Marketing

The most documented organic SEO machine in SaaS. ~50% of traffic from search. Top 3 positions for 30,000+ keywords. 4.8–5.5M monthly organic visitors.

Two pillars: (1) 38,612+ programmatic integration pair pages (e.g., "Gmail + Salesforce integration"), (2) 171 "Best Apps" editorial roundups (1.1M monthly visits).

Partners drive 40% of signups. Developer platform (Aug 2012) → 4,000+ app partners (from 800 in <5 years) → each partner brings own user base.

"Code Red" (Mar 2023 GPT-4 launch) → internal AI hackathon published as public thought leadership → 97% employee AI adoption story became widely shared.
