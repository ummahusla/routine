## Enterprise Adoption Survey Data

From the Zapier survey on AI agent adoption (https://finance.yahoo.com/news/zapier-survey-finds-84-enterprises-130000504.html, October 2025):

Survey of 525 U.S. C-Suite executives at companies with 1,000+ employees:
- **84%** plan to increase AI agent investments in the next 12 months
- **72%** are currently using or testing AI agents
- **40%** of companies using AI agents have multiple agents in production
- **47%** use AI agents for data management (entry and extraction)
- **49%** have deployed agents in Customer Support
- **47%** have deployed agents in Operations
- **30%** see automating routine workflows as the greatest potential use case
- **36%** reported increased security and privacy risks from disconnected AI tools

**Emily Mabie, AI Automation Engineer at Zapier:**
> "Enterprises are rushing to adopt AI agents because automating routine workflows is massive"

Survey methodology: 525 respondents, U.S. C-Suite executives, companies with 1,000+ employees, October 23–27, 2025, ±4% margin of error at 95% confidence level.
