### 1. Vodafone

**Industry:** Telecommunications / Cybersecurity
**Use Case:** Threat intelligence automation and SOAR capability

**Source:** https://n8n.io/case-studies/vodafone/

**Key Metrics:**
- 33 workflows launched (since August 2024)
- £2.2M cost avoided
- 5,000+ person-days saved
- ~£300k monthly savings in 2025
- Critical feed monitoring every 5 minutes with automated triage

**Background:**
- Vodafone UK is the largest telecommunications company in the United Kingdom
- Processes 3-5 billion events monthly with thousands of alerts
- Top 3 vulnerable sector to cyber attacks (after finance/healthcare)
- Average breach cost in telco: $5.72M
- Telecom Security Act (2024) requires expanded logging/monitoring
- Needed to increase log retention from 90 days to 13 months

**Solution:** Adopted n8n Enterprise with partner Bounteous as accelerator. Implemented modular workflow design and CI/CD lifecycle using n8n environments. Developed reusable workflows for fraud detection, IP geolocation, and security modules.

**Quotes:**

Claire Van Hinsbergh, Engineering Manager at Vodafone:
> "n8n provides SOAR capability and workflows in a low-code model"
> "We can monitor critical feeds every 5 minutes with instant alerts..."
> "n8n did everything we wanted, all in one tool. It allows us to work smarter rather than harder."

Sumit Sachdeva, Director, Bounteous:
> "n8n flexibility helped us design entire CI/CD DevOps lifecycle..."

**From n8n homepage:**
> "n8n provides SOAR capability and workflows in a low-code model" — Claire Van Hinsbergh, Cyber Operations Engineering Manager

### 2. Delivery Hero
