## Complete Funding History

### Seed Round — March 2020

Source: https://techcrunch.com/2020/03/13/n8n-a-fair-code-workflow-automation-platform-raises-seed-from-sequoia-as-vc-firm-steps-up-in-europe/

**Amount:** $1.5 million

**Lead investors:** UK's firstminute Capital and Sequoia Capital (co-led)

**Additional investors:**
- Runa Capital
- Tiny VC
- System.One
- Kevin Hartz (co-founder of Eventbrite & Xoom)
- Ilkka Paananen (co-founder of Supercell)
- Nan Li and Daniel Liem (Obvious Ventures)

**Sequoia context:** This was Sequoia's first seed investment in Germany. Sequoia hired Luciana Lixandru as first European partner (previously at Accel) and was continuing to invest from U.S. funds without launching Europe-specific funds.

**Sequoia partner quote:**
> Matthew Miller, Sequoia partner: "As talent is becoming more scarce, every organization is looking to get more from the great people they have. This is driving a surge in automation solutions in every industry."

**Wikipedia confirmation:** "March 2020: $1.5 million seed funding (Sequoia Capital, firstminute capital)"

---

### Series A — April 2021

Source: https://techcrunch.com/2021/04/26/n8n-raises-12m-for-its-fair-code-approach-to-low-code-workflow-automation/ and https://blog.n8n.io/series-a-announcement/

**Amount:** $12 million

**Lead investor:** Felicis Ventures

**Participating investors:** Sequoia Capital, firstminute Capital, Harpoon Ventures

**Total funding to date:** approximately $14 million

**Announcement date:** April 26, 2021

**Traction metrics at time of funding:**
- Community members: 16,000
- Team size: grew from 4 to 16 members in one year
- GitHub stargazers: Over 13,000
- New integrations added: 130+

**Felicis Ventures' Aydin Senkut quote:**
> "We feel that data is the new oil, and one of the special things here is not just low or no-code per se, but how n8n is making it seamless and easy to connect tens or even hundreds of apps."

**Jan Oberhauser Series A blog post:**
> "Jan is building a strong team with incredible momentum to bring n8n's next-generation open workflow automation solution to a growing community of developers across multiple industries." — Aydin Senkut, Founder and Managing Partner of Felicis Ventures

**Valuation:** Not disclosed at time of Series A

**Use of funds:** Expanding the team across engineering, developer relations, sales, and marketing; developing the product with new features

**Notable company practices mentioned:**
- $100 monthly stipend per employee for open source donations
- Quarterly hackathons for team building
- Plans for n8n 1.0 release, user management system, and certification courses

**Clarification note:** The Series A was led by Felicis Ventures, NOT Sequoia (Sequoia participated in the round but had led the seed).

### Series B — March 2025

Source: https://blog.n8n.io/series-b/ and https://techcrunch.com/2025/03/24/fair-code-pioneer-n8n-raises-60m-for-ai-powered-workflow-automation/ and https://www.highlandeurope.com/n8n-raises-e55-million-from-highland-europe-to-transform-workflow-automation-for-technical-teams-by-unifying-ai-code-and-human-building-blocks/

**Amount:** €55 million (approximately $60 million USD)

**Lead investor:** Highland Europe

**Co-investors:** HV Capital, Sequoia, Felicis, Harpoon

**Announcement date:** March 25, 2025 (per blog.n8n.io) / March 24, 2025 (TechCrunch)

**Valuation:** approximately €250–300 million ($270–350 million) — sources vary

From TechCrunch:
> Valuation: approximately €250 million ($270 million)

From Wikipedia:
> Valuation: €300 million

From the EU-Startups article:
> Valuation: €300 million

**Company metrics at time of Series B:**
- More than 3,000 enterprise customers
- Around 200,000–230,000 active users
- Over 70,000 stars on GitHub
- Revenues increased 5X after AI pivot in 2022
- Revenues doubled in the last two months (as of March 2025)
- Approximately 75% of customers now use AI tools

**Jan Oberhauser Series B quote:**
> "It's a prompt to build workflow. People don't really need to write 50 lines of code to integrate the functionality of, say, sending an email. Now in natural language, you can write 'get information from X and send it to Y.'"

> "This €55 million Series B is not the destination – it's fuel for the journey ahead"

> "Automation shouldn't be a black box – companies need transparency, customisation and cost effic..."

**Highland Europe Partner David Blyghton quote:**
> "n8n has built something truly special — an amazing team, an engaged and rapidly growing community..."

> "Everyone is trying to leverage AI but struggling to find practical use cases. The design, scale, and throughput of n8n is what allows people to adopt it."

**Use of funds:**
- Team expansion
- Education investment for beginner and advanced users
- Dedicated support and partnership with power users
- New offices opening in New York and London in 2025

**Named customers at Series B:**
- Vodafone
- Delivery Hero

**Strategic positioning:**
> "More than half user base located in U.S." (TechCrunch)

### Series C — October 2025

Source: https://blog.n8n.io/series-c/ and Bloomberg (https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-09/ai-agent-startup-n8n-nets-2-5-billion-valuation-with-backing-from-nvidia)

**Amount:** $180 million

**Total funding to date:** $240 million (per blog.n8n.io Series C announcement)

**Lead investor:** Accel

**Supporting investors:** Meritech, Redpoint, Evantic, Visionaries Club

**Corporate investors:** NVentures (NVIDIA's venture capital arm), T.Capital

**Previous backers (follow-on):** Felicis Ventures, Sequoia, Highland Europe, HV Capital

**Valuation:** $2.5 billion

**Announcement date:** October 9, 2025

**Growth metrics at time of Series C:**
- 6x user growth (in one year)
- 10x revenue growth (in one year)

**Jan Oberhauser Series C quote:**
> "The AI race isn't only about smarter models - it's about who can actually put that intelligence to work reliably, inside actual businesses."

**From TechFundingNews:**
> Valuation jumped from $350M (Series B) to $2.5B at Series C — in seven months

**Core platform philosophy at Series C:**
> Combines "AI, code, and humans in the same process, on the same platform"

**Recent feature additions:**
> Evaluations, Data Tables, and additional improvements noted in release notes

From the Accel podcast (https://www.accel.com/podcast-episodes/bonus-n8ns-jan-oberhauser-on-building-the-excel-of-ai):
> Episode host: Ben Fletcher (Accel Partner)
> Announcement: Accel leading n8n's Series C
