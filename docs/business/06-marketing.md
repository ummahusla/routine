## "Fair-Code" as a PR/Positioning Asset

From multiple sources, the term "fair-code" — coined by n8n — became a significant marketing and differentiation tool:

From Jan's blog post:
> "n8n become the first official fair-code project."

The faircode.io website created with Kenneth Malac (early 2020) became the reference resource for the concept.

From TechCrunch coverage:
- "n8n, a 'fair code' workflow automation platform, raises seed from Sequoia" (March 2020)

The "fair-code" label became part of n8n's standard press description across all funding rounds.
