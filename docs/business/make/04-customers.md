### 4. SERHANT. — Cut Automation Costs by 10x (Zapier → Make Migration)

**Source:** https://www.make.com/en/success-stories/make-helps-serhant-cut-automation-costs-and-scale-business

**Contact:** SERHANT. (US luxury real estate company); implementation by BuildLab (Make solution partner)

**Background:** SERHANT. initially used Zapier, but its pay-per-task model became unsustainable as operations grew. In 2020 they engaged BuildLab to migrate to Make.

**Key outcomes:**
- Reduced automation expenses by up to 10x by switching from Zapier to Make
- Kept a rapidly growing amount of form submissions tailored to each buyer/seller
- Onboarded hundreds of agents to the S.MPLE app with minimal resources
- Processed thousands of course enrollments automatically

**Three Make-powered systems built:**
1. Website form submissions routed via webhooks to Make (replacing generic autoresponders)
2. S.MPLE app onboarding integrated with Calendly via Make — autonomously schedules onboarding calls, sends tailored emails, creates/updates user profiles synced to CRM
3. Stripe payment verification for "Sell it like a SERHANT." course, connected via Make

**Verbatim description:** "Switching from Zapier to Make was a game-changer for SERHANT, allowing them to reduce automation costs by up to 10x, with Make's advanced capabilities enabling them to allocate resources to more strategic initiatives."
