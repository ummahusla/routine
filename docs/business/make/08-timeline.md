# Make.com (formerly Integromat) Chronological Timeline — Pre-2013 to Present

> Raw research dossier. All quotes are verbatim from primary sources where available. Every fact includes a source URL. Where dates are approximated from context clues, that is noted inline.

## Pre-2013 — Founders' Backgrounds

**Background (pre-2013)**
- Four of the eventual six co-founders — Roman Bršlica, Tomáš Schel, Michal Toman, and Pavel Duras — operate a company called **Integrators**, which rents programmers to large financial and industrial firms in the Czech Republic.
- Ondřej Gazda and Patrik Šimek work for the owner of the gaming portal **Game Park** (formerly Gamezone), which is owned by some of the same Integrators partners.
- The repeated, manual work of integrating enterprise software systems prompts the founders to discuss building a universal integration tool usable by business people — not just IT professionals — without requiring knowledge of a coding language.
- Source: CzechTheValley (https://www.czechthevalley.com/l/there-are-great-things-ahead-of-us-say-co-founders-of-integromat-czech-startup-acquired-by-celonis-for-over-100-million/); CzechStartups.gov.cz (https://czechstartups.gov.cz/en/novinky/interview-with-ondrej-gazda-and-patrik-simek-on-an-investment-that-resonates-with-the-startup-world-the-czech-company-integromat-was-acquired-by-the-german-firm-celonis-for-a-price-of-more-than-czk-2/)

## 2013 — Company Founded

**2013**
- **Integromat, s.r.o. founded** in Prague, Czech Republic as a project of the company **Cloud Service**. The platform is conceived to automate the repetitive integration work the founders' consulting businesses performed manually for banks and large enterprises.
- Six co-founders: **Ondřej Gazda** (President/CEO), **Patrik Šimek** (CTO), **Michal Toman** (Platform Engineer), **Pavel Duras** (CFO), **Tomáš Schel** (investor/co-owner), and **Roman Bršlica** (investor/co-owner).
- The company is bootstrapped — no outside funding is raised at any point before the 2020 acquisition.
- Source: CzechStartups.gov.cz interview with Ondřej Gazda and Patrik Šimek (https://czechstartups.gov.cz/en/novinky/interview-with-ondrej-gazda-and-patrik-simek-on-an-investment-that-resonates-with-the-startup-world-the-czech-company-integromat-was-acquired-by-the-german-firm-celonis-for-a-price-of-more-than-czk-2/); Tracxn (https://tracxn.com/d/companies/integromat/__0uq9kdCRGqnh8fjK828EpkH10XIp4ZUTS6DU1gcryu0)

> **Note on founding dates:** Sources conflict. Crunchbase and TechCrunch list founding as 2012; Tracxn and EverybodyWiki list 2016 (likely the public launch year); the Czech startup government interview and CzechTheValley reporting establish 2013 as when the company entity and internal platform work formally began, with 2015 cited as when the group formally decided to commercialize. The 2016 date reflects the public beta launch.

## 2016 — Public Launch

**~May 2016 (approximate)**
- **Integromat enters public beta.** An August 9, 2016 Medium post by co-founder Ondřej Gazda states: "After almost 3 months in public beta, we have reached 2000 registrations and received great feedback." This places the public beta launch approximately in May 2016.
- At the 2000-registration milestone, the platform supports packages including Mailgun, VTiger CRM, and Global Payments Webpay, with Todoist, Gitter, and SugarCRM/SuiteCRM in development.
- Over 400 new scenario templates released to help users.
- Source: Ondřej Gazda on Medium — "What happened in the last few weeks at Integromat" (https://medium.com/integromat/what-happend-in-a-last-few-weeks-at-integromat-e19ff573ad8f); published August 9, 2016.

**~Mid-June 2016**
- **Routers** introduced as a core differentiating feature — allowing data to be duplicated and split into multiple branches within a scenario, each processed separately.
- Source: Ondřej Gazda on Medium — "Routers" (https://medium.com/integromat/routers-f3dc8a23b825)

**August 12, 2016**
- **Hacker News submission: "Integromat – A tool for automating manual processes"** (item ID: 12268380). Discussion covers 105+ services supported, integrated support for MySQL, MS SQL, and PostgreSQL databases, and comparison with IFTTT and Microsoft automation tools.
- Source: Hacker News (https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=12268380)

**~October 2016**
- **U.S. bank integration launched.** Integromat becomes one of the first automation services to support connections with U.S. banks, enabling cashflow automation.
- Source: Ondřej Gazda on Medium — "Integromat can work with U.S. banks. Automate your cashflow!" (https://medium.com/integromat/integromat-can-work-with-u-s-banks-automate-your-cashflow-5dd2ac48c920)

**~November 2016**
- Scenario editor redesigned: bold font for package names, enlarged circles on connecting lines.
- Source: Ondřej Gazda on Medium — "News on Integromat" (https://medium.com/integromat/news-on-integromat-a915624f7d4f)

**~December 2016**
- **SOAP protocol support** announced on Hacker News (item ID: 13168111), December 2016.

## 2020 — $10M Revenue, Celonis Acquisition

**April 2020**
- **$10 million ARR milestone reached.** GetLatka interview with Ondřej Gazda confirms Integromat hit $10M revenue with 250,000 customers, entirely bootstrapped — no outside investment.
- Source: GetLatka (https://getlatka.com/companies/integromat)

**~Q3 2020**
- Integromat begins releasing **one new app integration per day**, launching 270 new apps during 2020 — outpacing Microsoft Power Automate, which released approximately 100 new apps that year.
- Source: Make 2020 Year in Review (https://www.make.com/en/blog/integromat-2020-in-review)

**April 2020 (approximate)**
- **COVID-19 tracking app** launched on Integromat, pulling data from Johns Hopkins University. Listed on Product Hunt, receiving approximately 129 upvotes.
- Source: Product Hunt (https://www.producthunt.com/products/integromat)

**October 14, 2020**
- **Celonis acquires Integromat.** Announcement at 4:30 PM CET, Prague/Munich/New York City.
- Acquisition price: **"over $100 million"** — Celonis co-founder and CEO **Alexander Rinke**: "a three-digit amount of over $100 million." Czech sources report the amount as **more than CZK 2.5 billion** (~$107M at 2020 exchange rates). One secondary source cites $107,644,175.
- Integromat had **rejected multiple prior acquisition approaches**, including from large SaaS companies, before Celonis made an offer the founders accepted. Reports indicate Celonis had approached Integromat multiple times before the deal closed.
- Celonis CEO Alexander Rinke, quoted at announcement: Integromat acquisition is intended "to accelerate move to process automation."
- Founders (Gazda and Šimek) remain to manage the company under the acquisition.
- Legal advisors: Schoenherr (for Celonis); Davis Polk (additional counsel).
- Source: TechCrunch (https://techcrunch.com/2020/10/14/celonis-acquires-czech-startup-integromat-to-accelerate-move-to-process-automation/); PRNewswire (https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/celonis-acquires-integromat-an-industry-leading-online-automation-platform-301152316.html); CzechTheValley (https://www.czechthevalley.com/l/there-are-great-things-ahead-of-us-say-co-founders-of-integromat-czech-startup-acquired-by-celonis-for-over-100-million/); CzechStartups.gov.cz (https://czechstartups.gov.cz/en/novinky/interview-with-ondrej-gazda-and-patrik-simek-on-an-investment-that-resonates-with-the-startup-world-the-czech-company-integromat-was-acquired-by-the-german-firm-celonis-for-a-price-of-more-than-czk-2/); Mergr (https://mergr.com/transaction/celonis-se-acquires-integromat-s.r.o.); Schoenherr (https://www.schoenherr.eu/content/czech-republic-schoenherr-advises-celonis-se-on-acquisition-of-integromat-group/); Davis Polk (https://www.davispolk.com/experience/celonis-acquisition-integromat)

## 2022 — Rebrand to Make, New Platform Launch

**February 22, 2022**
- **Integromat officially rebrands to Make.** BusinessWire press release announces: "Today, no-code integration platform Integromat, launched 'Make'."
- Make is launched as **its own business unit within Celonis**, approximately one and a half years after the October 2020 acquisition.
- New brand vision: "Unlock the full potential of people by giving them the freedom to create visually, build, and automate at the speed of their ideas."
- New product platform features: entirely new UI with vibrant pink/purple gradient branding replacing Integromat's blue/gray; parallel webhook execution by default; community template creation (previously Integromat-employees-only); new Teams feature for access control.
- **Integromat.com** to remain as legacy product through 2022; all existing customers offered seamless upgrade path to Make.
- Make.com powers over **500,000+ organizations** globally at launch.
- Source: BusinessWire (https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20220222005231/en/Integromat-Evolves-to-Make-Expanding-Its-Vision-to-Empower-Creators-to-Innovate-Without-Limits); Make.com (https://www.make.com/en/integromat-evolves-to-make)

**June 9, 2022**
- **Make announces AWS Partnership** — Make Enterprise product listed on AWS Marketplace. AWS customers can procure Make's visual automation platform and pay via their AWS bill.
- At launch: 1,000+ available apps including Workday, Greenhouse, ServiceNow, and Salesforce; 6,000+ endpoints.
- Source: Make (https://www.make.com/en/aws-make-partnership)

**2022 (year)**
- Over **2 billion scenario runs** on the platform during 2022.

## 2025 — Make AI Agents, MCP, Make Grid GA, Waves '25

**April 14, 2025**

- AI Agents use natural language to understand goals, make context-aware decisions, and take action across 30,000+ available actions — without writing code. "Workflows that think, decide, and adapt."
- At launch: More than **200,000 businesses** using Make worldwide.
- Source: PRNewswire (https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/make-launches-make-ai-agents-adding-real-time-intelligence-to-no-code-automation-302426790.html); Make press release (https://www.make.com/en/make-ai-agents-press-release); Make blog (https://www.make.com/en/blog/make-ai-agents)

**April 22, 2025**
- **Scenario Outputs** feature launched — enables structured data output from Make scenarios, allowing AI agents to receive clean, usable data. Key complement to MCP integration.
- Source: Make blog — "Improve AI automations with structured data in scenario outputs" (https://www.make.com/en/blog/introducing-scenario-outputs)

**May 16, 2025**
- **Make MCP (Model Context Protocol) Server launched.** Cloud-based gateway connecting AI agents (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, etc.) to Make scenarios through the standardized MCP protocol — without managing API endpoints, installing local code, or maintaining infrastructure.
- Unlike most MCP servers requiring local setup, Make's implementation requires only generating a token and pasting a URL into supported AI tool configuration spaces.
- Source: Make blog — "What is MCP Server? | Model Context Protocol For AI Agents | Make" (https://www.make.com/en/blog/model-context-protocol-mcp-server); Make Developer Hub (https://developers.make.com/mcp-server)

**June 2025**
- **Make AI Content Extractor** launched in open beta — built-in AI module that extracts and summarizes data from images, PDFs, and audio files within scenarios, without requiring third-party tools or external APIs.
- Source: Make blog — "Introducing Make AI Content Extractor" (https://www.make.com/en/blog/make-ai-content-extractor); CloudCache Consulting (https://cloudcacheconsulting.com/news/2-major-releases-from-makecom-in-june-2025-that-will-transform-automation-workflows)

**June 24, 2025**
- **Make Grid reaches General Availability** — open to all Make customers after closed beta since late 2024. Enhanced GA version includes 40+ new app connectors, specialized SQL parsing, efficiency monitoring layers (credit and data consumption tracking), and auto-highlighted assets requiring attention.
- Source: CloudCache Consulting (https://cloudcacheconsulting.com/news/2-major-releases-from-makecom-in-june-2025-that-will-transform-automation-workflows); Make press releases (https://www.make.com/en/press)

**October 15–16, 2025**
- **Waves '25** — Make's annual conference at Bergson Kunstkraftwerk in Munich. Over **700 Make enthusiasts** from around the world attend. 16 product-related updates and 17 structured sessions.

  - **Next-generation Make AI Agents** — agents brought directly into the scenario builder canvas; new **Reasoning Panel** showing real-time agent thinking, tool calls, and decision paths; new **Library of Agents** with ready-made templates; file support and conversation history; evolved UI for faster building and reusable agents.

## Funding History

**No external funding raised pre-acquisition.** Integromat was entirely bootstrapped from 2013 through the October 2020 Celonis acquisition. No venture rounds, no angel investment on record. Reached $10M ARR with 250,000 customers as a self-funded company.

Post-acquisition, Make operates as a business unit of Celonis SE. Celonis itself raised approximately **$1.77 billion** in total venture funding (including a $1B Series D in 2021 at a $11B valuation), but Make does not have separate external funding.

- Source: GetLatka (https://getlatka.com/companies/integromat); Crunchbase — Integromat (https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/integromat-2); Celonis Wikipedia (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Celonis)
